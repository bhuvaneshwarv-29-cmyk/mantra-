Our standard package pricing includes accommodation, transfers, breakfasts, local guide tours, and entry tickets. Flight ticket booking is optional and can be added based on your departure city.

Cancellations made 30 days prior to the travel date qualify for a 90% refund. For cancellations between 15 to 29 days, we offer a 50% refund. Cancellations under 14 days are non-refundable but can be rescheduled for a minor fee.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, net banking, UPI, and bank wire transfers. We also offer easy 3-month interest-free installment plans for selected destinations.
